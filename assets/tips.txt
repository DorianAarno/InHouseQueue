Enable or Disable duo queue with `/admin duo_queue [enable/disable]`
Enable or Disable MMR with `/admin sbmm [enable/disable]`
If someone is AFK during ready up phase, remove them with `/admin user_dequeue [user]`
Give helpers permissions to run some commands! `/admin enable [role] [command]`
Wrong winning team result? `/admin change_winner [gameid] [team]`
Look at your own rank with `/rank [options]`
Your MMR will be visible after 10 placement matches with SBMM enabled.
